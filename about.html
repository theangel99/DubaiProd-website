About Dubai Prod

Building Brands Across Borders

We're a global PR and digital agency on a mission to help ambitious brands tell their stories, reach their audiences, and achieve exceptional growth.

Our Global Journey

What started as a boutique agency in Slovenia has evolved into a global force in PR and digital communications, with teams and projects spanning four continents.

Our expansion into Dubai, the USA, and Turkey wasn't just about geography — it was about understanding diverse markets, cultures, and audiences. This global perspective, combined with deep local expertise, is what sets us apart.

Today, we serve clients ranging from luxury real estate developers to innovative tech startups, healthcare providers to hospitality brands. What unites them all is a commitment to excellence and a desire to stand out in their markets.
