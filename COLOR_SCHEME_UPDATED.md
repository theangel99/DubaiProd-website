## Color Palette

### Background Colors (Deep Black & Charcoal)
- **Primary Background**: `#0A0A0A` - Deep black for maximum contrast
- **Secondary Background**: `#111111` - Slightly lighter black
- **Tertiary Background**: `#1A1A1A` - Subtle variation
- **Card Background**: `#151515` - Cards stand out subtly
- **Hover Background**: `#1F1F1F` - Interactive state

### Gold Accent Colors (Dubai Premium)
- **Primary Gold**: `#D4AF37` - Classic, rich gold
- **Light Champagne Gold**: `#F4E4B0` - Softer, elegant highlight
- **Deep Gold**: `#B8941E` - Rich, darker gold
- **Metallic Gold**: `#FFD700` - Bright metallic highlight

### Text Colors (High Contrast White to Gray)
- **Primary Text**: `#FFFFFF` - Pure white for headings
- **Secondary Text**: `#E5E5E5` - Off-white for body

- **Border**: `#252525` - Subtle dark borders

## Design Philosophy

The new color scheme embodies:
- **Dubai Luxury**: Rich gold evokes premium hospitality and excellence
- **Modern Premium**: Deep blacks create sophisticated, high-end aesthetic
- **High Contrast**: White text on black ensures readability
- **Subtle Elegance**: Gold used sparingly as accent, not overwhelmingly
- **International Appeal**: Timeless colors that work across cultures

## Visual Hierarchy

1. **Gold = Action & Importance**
   - Primary CTAs
   - Key metrics
   - Important highlights

2. **White = Primary Content**
   - Headings
   - Important text
   - Navigation

3. **Grays = Supporting Content**
   - Body text
   - Descriptions
   - Secondary information

4. **Black = Canvas**
   - Backgrounds
   - Cards
   - Containers
