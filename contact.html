Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a full range of PR and digital marketing services including social media management, video production, 3D & CGI, SEO, web development, ads management, PR & media relations, and branding. We can handle individual services or create integrated campaigns.

Do you work with businesses of all sizes?

Yes! We work with everyone from startups to established enterprises. Our approach is tailored to your specific needs, goals, and budget. Whether you need a comprehensive marketing strategy or help with a specific project, we can help.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project scope. A branding project might take 8-12 weeks, a website rebuild 6-10 weeks, and ongoing services like social media or SEO are typically managed on a monthly retainer basis. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

How do you measure success?

We align our metrics with your business goals. Depending on the project, we might track website traffic, lead generation, conversion rates, social media engagement, media coverage, or revenue impact. We provide transparent reporting and regular strategy reviews.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing depends on the scope and complexity of your project. We offer both project-based pricing and monthly retainers for ongoing services. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific needs.

Can you work with our existing team or agencies?

Absolutely. We're experienced at collaborating with in-house teams and complementing existing agency relationships. We can fill specific gaps in your capabilities or take on discrete projects while working alongside your team.
